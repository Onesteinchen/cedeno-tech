Privacy policy

Last updated: 20 July 2026

This policy explains how personal data is processed when you use the Cedeno Technologies website.

1. Controller

Cedeno Technologies is responsible for the processing described on this website. Privacy enquiries and requests can be sent to info@cedeno-tech.de.

2. Data, purposes and legal bases

Website delivery. The site is hosted through GitHub Pages. Technical server logs may include IP address, request time, requested page, referrer, browser and device information. Processing is necessary to deliver and protect the website and is based on our legitimate interests under Article 6(1)(f) GDPR.

Contact form. If you send an enquiry, the form processes your name, email address, customer type, product interest, message, selected language and source page. The data is sent through Google Apps Script and stored in Google Sheets so Cedeno can answer your enquiry and discuss an R2 project. The legal basis is Article 6(1)(b) GDPR for pre-contractual communication and Article 6(1)(f) GDPR for general business enquiries.

Language and consent preferences. The site stores cedeno-lang and cedeno-cookie-consent in your browser's localStorage. These values remember your language and privacy choice. Necessary storage supports the requested website function. Optional analytics would require consent under Article 6(1)(a) GDPR. The current site does not load an analytics provider.

3. Recipients and service providers

Data may be processed by GitHub for hosting and security, and by Google for the contact-form endpoint and Google Sheets storage. When you open LinkedIn, Instagram or YouTube from this site, the selected platform processes data under its own privacy policy.

4. Processing outside the EEA

Some service providers may process data outside the European Economic Area. Where this occurs, the provider is responsible for using an applicable transfer mechanism and safeguards required by data-protection law.

5. Storage period

Enquiry data is kept only as long as needed to answer the enquiry, prepare or manage a business relationship, and meet applicable statutory retention duties. Browser preferences remain until you change them or delete the relevant localStorage entries.

6. Your rights

Subject to the legal conditions, you may request access, correction, deletion, restriction, data portability, or object to processing under Articles 15 to 21 GDPR. You may withdraw consent at any time with effect for the future and lodge a complaint with a competent supervisory authority.

7. Sensitive data and security

Please do not submit health information or other sensitive personal data through the contact form. We use proportionate technical and organisational measures, but no internet transmission can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

8. Changes and contact

We may update this policy when the website or its data processing changes. You can reopen the consent panel through “Cookie settings” in the homepage footer. For questions, rights requests, or withdrawal of consent, email info@cedeno-tech.de.

Bereitstellung der Website. Die Website wird über GitHub Pages gehostet. Die Verarbeitung dient der sicheren Bereitstellung der Website und beruht auf unserem berechtigten Interesse gemäß Art. 6 Abs. 1 lit. f DSGVO.

Die aktuelle Website lädt keinen Analysedienst.
